Rio2C

Rio2C is the largest audiovisual, music and innovation event in Latin America. Rio2C is a festival of creative minds that gather together for 6 days to experience n unique mix of content, to improve knowledge, network and make big and innovative deals. Invited by RIo2C’s CEO Rafael Lazarini, Zé Ricardo created the whole music concept for the event. The music panels - a new model of pitching concerts with a music commission and Festivalia bringing together independent festivals from all over the country - were the pillar of the event's music curation. The musical production of Rio2C was accomplished by executive director of Leve, Fabiane Costa.
