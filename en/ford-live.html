Put high quality samba, hip hop and MPB in a pot. Mix them all with care. Serve and enjoy without moderation”. That was the recipe for the Live's success that brought together Diogo Nogueira, Maria Rita and Rael in a music concert that lasted more than two hours. Zé Ricardo assembled an amazing music group to play with them, and together with his maestro Maurício Piassarollo created the arrangement for the songs played at the Live. In addition to their own hits, Diogo, Maria and Rael performed classical Brazilian songs by Vinicius de Moraes, Djavan and others. Always with new and modern arrangements. Zé created this Live as a request of the art director of Banco de Eventos, Fernanda Abujanrra, for annual Ford’s Live
