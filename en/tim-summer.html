Tim Summer

During the months of January and February 2020, TIM Summer reigned on Ipanema’s beach in Rio de Janeiro, gathering more than six hundred thousand people over its nine days. Zé Ricardo created the whole artistic concept of the event, invited by Rafaello Ramundo, CEO of the agency Novo Traço. The concept created by Zé included concerts + parties. First there was a big concert with great names from Brazilian music scenery, like Anavitória, Iza, Melim, Baco Exu do Blues, Elza Soares, Criolo among others. Right after each concert, a renowned party took center stage, making the evenings even more magical, and exciting for the audience that did not want to leave. In addition to music, “Tim Summer” celebrated diversity with days dedicated to racial equality and the LGBTQ + rights, becoming part of Rio’s public’s memory.
